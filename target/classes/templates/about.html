ABOUT

Maple Movies® and the Maplemeter® score are the most trusted recommendation sources for quality entertainment. As the leading online aggregator of movie and TV show reviews from professional critics, Maple Movies offers the most comprehensive guide to what's Fresh. The world famous Maple Meter score represents the percentage of positive professional reviews for films and TV shows and is used by millions of fans to help with their viewing decisions. Maple Movies designates the best-reviewed movies and TV shows as Certified Fresh™. That accolade is awarded with a Maplemeter score of 75% and higher and a required minimum number of reviews. In 2016, Flixster and Maple Movies became part of Fandango's portfolio of digital properties, serving moviegoers and entertainment fans.
